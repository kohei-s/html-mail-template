The preheader text provides a short preview that appears alongside the subject line in the inbox. It gives recipients a quick glimpse of the email content and works with the subject line to encourage opens. Though visible in the inbox view, it is hidden when the email is opened, typically using a hidden div style in HTML. For best results across both desktop and mobile email clients, the preheader text should be between 40 and 130 characters long.
